    Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the rental process work?

    Renting a game is easy and convenient. Customers can browse through our collection, select the games they want to rent, and complete the checkout process. We will then deliver the games to the customer's doorstep. The rental period and the prices vary depending on the type of rental plan.

  • How do I return a rented game?

    Returning a rented game is easy. We will provide instructions on how to return the game when you receive it, and we'll also include a prepaid shipping label.

  • How can I pay for my rental or purchase?

    We accept all major credit cards and debit cards as well as PayPal. We also accept payments through mobile wallet services too.

  • How can I contact customer support?

    You can contact customer support by phone, email, or through the contact form on our website. We are always here to help and make your experience with us a positive one.

  • Are there any discounts or promotions available?

    We offer different discounts and promotions from time to time, which we advertise on our website. Be sure to follow us on social media to stay informed about the latest offers and deals.
